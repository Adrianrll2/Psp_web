• Clean fonts like Frutiger, Segoe UI, Helvetica.(not standard)

• Bright colors: blues, greens, whites, silvers.

• Common elements: • Water droplets, leaves, sunlight, wind. • Glass-like surfaces, reflections, lighting flares. • Transparent UI panels and shiny icons.

Conclusion

Frutiger Aero is the result of combining glossy design, skeuomorphism, and the Aero UI style of Windows, shaped by corporate optimism and eco-futurism. It reflects a period of visual design where nature and technology were blended to create a shiny, idealized digital world.
